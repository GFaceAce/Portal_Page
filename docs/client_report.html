Client Report

Q. How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?
A. I chose Pablo Picasso because I had learned a lot about him when I was a young child, so it was very interesting to learn about him again. I chose two of his most famous pieces to highlight, and also included a summary of his life.
Q. How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?
A. I communicated with the lead developer primarily through email. He informed me that neither of his jr developers had done any of the work for their subpages.
Q. Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?
A. Aside from the missing subpages, the website looks exactly how I wanted it to look and I am quite pleased.
Q. What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?
A. Next time I 'hire' a team to do a project for me, I will be communicating much more frequently with them to make sure that everything is completed within the deadline.

Lead Developer Report

Q. How did you manage your development team and comunicate the tasks for your junior devs?
A. I emailed my jr devs every week to let them know what they were expected to work on. I also spoke with them in person to make sure that they had received the emails and knew what to do.
Q. How did you communicate with your client? Was there any issues?
A. I was able to speak with my client in person. He was able to clarify any questions that I had about his wireframe.
Q. How did you handle publishing your clients site and sharing it with the client?
A. My client had already published it through Github on his own.
Q. How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?
A. It was fairly easy to add and sync changes to Github, though resolving duplicate changes could be a little frusterating.

General Experience and Reflections

Q. What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?
A. Probably the most challenging part of this was overcoming my ADHD because the deadlines for each step were at the end of each week, so my brain told me to put it off until the last possible second.
Q. What did you enjoy most about this project?
A. I really enjoyed making websites for a variety of topics.
Q. What would you do differently if you could do the project again?
A. I would try to start early and stay ahead, so that I could complete it on time.
Q. Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.
A. I feel much more confident with real-world projects now because I have an experience of team leaders, delegation, deadlines, and clients.
